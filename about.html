Our Story

In the picturesque town of Littleton, Colorado, nestled against the backdrop of the Rocky Mountains, a group of ambitious friends gathered with a shared passion for gaming. They had grown up exploring digital worlds, from the pixelated landscapes of early RPGs to the intricate universes of modern adventures. Inspired by their collective love for gaming and driven by a desire to create something uniquely their own, they decided to embark on an audacious journey‚Äîto establish their very own gaming company. The year was 2013, and the gaming industry was booming with innovation and creativity. Undeterred by the challenges ahead, this group of friends pooled their resources and rented a small office space in downtown Littleton. Armed with laptops, sketches, and a fervent determination to make their mark, they began brainstorming and prototyping their first game ideas. The early days were filled with excitement and late-night brainstorming sessions fueled by local coffee and enthusiasm. They collaborated on every aspect of game development, from coding and design to storytelling and art direction. Without a name yet, they simply referred to themselves as "The Littleton Game Collective," embodying their commitment to the local community and their shared creative vision. Their breakthrough came with their debut title‚Äîa whimsical puzzle adventure set in a fictionalized version of Littleton itself. Drawing inspiration from the town's charming streets, historic landmarks, and natural beauty, they crafted a game that captured the essence of their hometown while offering players a delightful journey through imaginative puzzles and engaging narratives. As word spread about their innovative approach and heartfelt dedication, The Littleton Game Collective began to gain recognition within the indie gaming community. Their game received positive reviews for its creativity, attention to detail, and nostalgic charm, establishing them as a promising new force in the industry. Encouraged by their initial success, the collective expanded their team, welcoming local talent who shared their passion for storytelling and game design. Together, they continued to develop new projects that pushed the boundaries of indie gaming, from experimental VR experiences to narrative-driven adventures that resonated with players around the world. By 2017, The Littleton Game Collective had solidified its presence in the gaming scene, earning accolades for their commitment to innovation and community engagement. They hosted workshops for aspiring game developers, collaborated with local schools on educational initiatives, and participated in gaming conventions to showcase their work and connect with fellow creators. As they looked towards the future, The Littleton Game Collective remained dedicated to their core values of creativity, collaboration, and community. With each new project, they sought to inspire and entertain players while continuing to celebrate the unique spirit of their hometown. Today, The Littleton Game Collective stands as a testament to the power of passion and perseverance in pursuing one's dreams. What began as a humble gathering of friends in a small office space has evolved into a thriving hub of creativity and innovation, where every game they create reflects their shared love for storytelling, exploration, and the magic of gaming. As they continue their journey, they remain committed to pushing the boundaries of what indie games can achieve and creating experiences that leave a lasting impression on players worldwide.
